Feed Old People

  • Goal: $890

AppNomu through AppNomu Charity extends love, care, and support to old vulnerable people through our feeding program. We donate food parcels to old people, which include Rice, sugar, maize flour, wheat, cooking oils, soap for maintaining their health, and bread. Feeding old people boosts their health and immunity and is our priority to see strong old people in our community.
