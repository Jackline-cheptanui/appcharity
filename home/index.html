We believe talent is a source of income and one of the events that change, motivate, and inspire the lives of others. Skilling Her

  • Goal: $890
  • Raised: $390

AppNomu skills women in coding with basic coding skills. AppNomu focuses on women in coding and fintech and this year 2023 we are focusing on making sure that women, higher percentages participate in coding and emerge in technology. This year we will donate $5,500 to women who do coding and make sure that they are given good tools to make their dreams and projects. We believe that when we absorb women in coding, we can power their dreams and we envision a world where women are proportionally represented as technical leaders, executives, founders, VCs, board members, and software engineers
